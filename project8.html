App

Project Info:

The app is created using the Google Sheets and Glide and is a outcome of curiosity. The app uses 500+ rows long dataset for its functioning. The app display the lists of courses with their details available online. The app is made to help people wether they are students, profesionals, employees or anyone else to find course that suits them, develop the skills they want or enhance their previous skills. You can decide, check, compare from the list of courses the app shows. The app doesn't demand any permission or save any detail just instead of your reviews.

Features:
Login into the app can be done using Email and pin or using your google account.
Homepage displays different courses list with the course feild and course level .
On clicking on any item in the list of courses available the card with the details about the course like the duration of course, price of course, students already enrolled, type and level of course etc opens, the card also has three buttons to visit the course online, share with friends and to provide the review.
The app provides the feasibility to the user to easily search any course using the search bar, filter the courses based on the course levels, sort courses according to subjects, price, level.
The course reviews can be seen below the provide review button.
The users can see his/her reviews on different courses in the reviews tab.
