Visualization

Project Info:

The visualization is about the sales of goods in different cities. The viewer can categorized their view by profit value and can also view specific city. In first sheet it has the map of country by cities with their sales listed, on clicking on one city the webpage containing whole information will open in this case which is wikipedia.The second sheet is sales by month of different items. The next three sheets are hyperlinked, shows sales divided by categories and on clicking on one category its sub-category can be seen on next sheet, on clicking on sub-category the shipping mode on next sheet opens. Last sheet has all the data in one place with the count of items with their type sold in each country. Than we have dasboard to view all sheets at a glance.
